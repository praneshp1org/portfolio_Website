Intro

Hi there! I’m a fourth-year engineering student at the Institute of Engineering, Pulchowk Campus, with a passion for building intuitive and user-friendly mobile applications as a Flutter Developer. I enjoy turning ideas into seamless digital experiences.

On the cloud side of things, I specialize in designing scalable and secure solutions using AWS. From setting up CI/CD pipelines with tools like AWS CodePipeline, CodeBuild, and CodeDeploy to crafting event-driven serverless architectures, I focus on making development smoother and delivery faster.

I’m also a fan of automating workflows. With Terraform and Terraform Cloud, I create scalable, versioned environments, and with Kubernetes, I manage containerized applications at scale. Tools like Helm and ArgoCD help me simplify deployments and keep everything consistent using GitOps principles.

Beyond that, I love working with Python to build backend services and write automation scripts that bridge systems and scale with ease. Whether it’s managing SQL/NoSQL databases or ensuring systems are secure and monitored effectively, I’m all about delivering reliable, high-quality solutions.

I’m always excited to connect, collaborate, and bring innovative ideas to life. Let’s create something amazing together!
